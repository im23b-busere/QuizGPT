Data Collection & Use

Data Collection & Use

QuizGPT collects minimal personal information to provide our service:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we store your email address and a secure password hash for authentication purposes.
  • Usage Data: We track your quiz attempts and subscription status to enforce usage limits and provide premium features.
  • Payment Information: For premium subscriptions, payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. We only store your Stripe customer ID, not your payment details.

Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your information. Your quiz preferences and settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage.local API.

Third-party Services

  • Stripe: Payment processing is handled by Stripe. Their privacy policy applies to payment-related data.
  • OpenAI: Quiz questions are processed through OpenAI's API to generate answers. No personal data is shared with OpenAI beyond the quiz content.
