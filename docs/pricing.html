❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my premium subscription at any time?

+

Yes! You can cancel anytime by clicking manage subscription under the account tab in the extension. Your premium will stay active until the end of your billing period.

Does Premium auto-renew?

+

Yes, premium subscriptions automatically renew monthly to ensure uninterrupted service. You can cancel at any time to stop future renewals.

Which payment methods are supported?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and debit cards through our secure Stripe payment processor.

How many free attempts do I get?

+

You get 5 free quiz attempts with the free plan. After that, you'll need to upgrade to premium for up to 200 requests per month, or choose Ultra for unlimited requests.
